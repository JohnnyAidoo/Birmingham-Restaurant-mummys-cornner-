about us

Welcome To Mummy's Corner

Afro Restaurant Birmingham is the touch of Ghana situated in the heart of London city. Our restaurant blends a relaxed atmosphere with delicious authentic Ghanaian food achieved by using only the finest and freshest ingredients. Come and enjoy our extensive modern menu of Ghanaian dishes, with imported African beers, tropical cocktails, fine wine and champagne.

Bookings can be made throughout lunch and dinner for parties of up to 10 guests. You are also welcome to drop in anytime, and order drinks if you have to wait, or leave your number so we can call you when your seats are ready. Reserve your table now using the button below. You can also contact us on info@afrobirmingham.co.uk or call 0247 700 6427.
